God has a plan for all of us. This passage stands out to me because I know I do not have to worry about my future. He knows everything and what will be best for my will. For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."
Jeremiah 29:11
