When visiting San Francisco, food should be your least concern. From cheap eats to fine dining, this city has it all. The most common food you will see and smell is fish. Seafood restaurants are placed all over the city especially in Fisherman's Wharf. Not a fish fan? No problem because they also have basically any other food imaginable. Including LOTS of Chinese food in Chinatown. Food prices vary but you can definitely find a place to fit your food and budget needs.
